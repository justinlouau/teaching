Hi there, welcome to my teaching site! 

I'm Justin, an Academic Tutor at the [University of Sydney](https://www.sydney.edu.au/). I currently teach tutorials and labs for the [School of Computer Science](https://www.sydney.edu.au/engineering/schools/school-of-computer-science.html).

This site is currently an experimental way of sharing resources and quick links for my teaching.

If you need to get into contact with me, please send me an email at <a href = "mailto: justin.lou@sydney.edu.au">justin.lou@sydney.edu.au</a>. [Feedback](/feedback) is also much appreciated!
